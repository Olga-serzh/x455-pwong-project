The Science Behind Pain

Understanding Migraines: The Science Behind the Pain

Migraines aren’t just headaches — they’re complex neurological events that can be triggered by a wide range of internal and external factors. From changes in sleep and hormone levels to environmental stressors like weather, light, and diet, every person’s migraine pattern is different.

AssistWell is built on the science of migraine management. By tracking your body’s health metrics and daily activities, it helps uncover how your unique lifestyle may influence the frequency and intensity of your migraines. The goal isn’t just to react to symptoms — it’s to get ahead of them. With consistent data and AI-powered insights, you can start connecting the dots and making informed choices that support better days.

Reference: Mayo Clinic: Understanding Migraines

Your Body Knows First: Understanding Early Warning Signs of Migraines

Our bodies often signal imbalances long before we recognize them — subtle changes in heart rate, sleep quality, body temperature, or blood sugar levels can all hint at an oncoming migraine. AssistWell captures these signals through continuous monitoring, helping you identify early warning signs. By recognizing patterns in your body’s data, you can take proactive steps to reduce the severity or even prevent an episode altogether.

These early cues are especially valuable for individuals whose migraines are triggered by physiological stress or hormonal shifts. With the right insights, users can shift from reactive to preventative care.

Reference: Cleveland Clinic: Migraine Headaches

The Role of Lifestyle in Migraine Management

While genetics and neurological factors play a role in migraines, lifestyle habits often act as the tipping point. Irregular sleep, skipped meals, high stress, dehydration, and even extended screen time can all influence the likelihood and severity of an episode. What makes these triggers especially challenging is how sub tle and personal they can be — what affects one person may not affect another.

AssistWell helps bring clarity by encouraging consistent logging of your daily routines and behaviors, while its wearable sensor tracks your body’s response in real time. Over time, patterns emerge that help you understand how your lifestyle may be impacting your migraines. With better awareness comes better decision-making — and the ability to take back control of your day before a migraine takes over.

Reference: American Headache Society: Lifestyle Modification for Migraine
