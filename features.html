Questions? We've got you covered!

AssistWell is a personalized migraine management system that combines a wearable sensor, an intuitive mobile app, and smart AI insights. It helps you track symptoms, identify triggers, and connect with your care team, all in one place. AssistWell is designed to fit naturally into your daily routine without disrupting it. Whether you’re newly diagnosed or have been living with migraines for years, AssistWell gives you the tools to better understand your health and take control of your care.

Not at all. The AssistSensor is designed to be comfortable and completely non-invasive. It attaches gently to your skin with a medical-grade adhesive, similar to a small fitness patch. Most users say they barely notice it during daily activities — including sleep, exercise, and work. You can apply or remove it easily at home without needing a doctor.

If you experience migraines and want a smarter, easier way to manage them, AssistWell may be a great fit. It’s especially helpful for people who want to understand their personal migraine patterns and reduce surprise attacks. Whether you track your symptoms already or are just getting started, AssistWell adapts to your lifestyle and health needs. It's also ideal for those working with a healthcare provider, as it helps bring real data into the conversation.

AssistWell offers a complete system for managing migraines — from real-time symptom tracking to personalized health insights. By wearing the AssistSensor and logging daily activity in the app, you start to see patterns that help prevent future attacks. The app’s AI helps connect the dots between things like sleep, stress, hydration, and environment. Plus, it creates easy-to-share reports for your care team, so your treatment can be more informed and proactive.

Coverage for AssistSensor depends on your insurance provider and plan. In some cases, it may be reimbursable through health savings accounts (HSA) or flexible spending accounts (FSA). We recommend checking with your insurance company or contacting our support team for guidance on your options. AssistWell is also working with healthcare partners to expand accessibility and coverage. Even without insurance, we offer different membership levels to fit various budgets.
